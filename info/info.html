Simple Violin Tuner

How to use

  • Just press the button you want to tune.
  • Press the button again to stop tuning.
  • or Press another button to tune other strings.

Customization

You can customize your tune tone if you want.

You need to turn on the dev mode to customize - (chrome://extensions/)

The tone files named with tone#.mp3 placed in /tones directory. You may try replacing these tone files with the templates below.
